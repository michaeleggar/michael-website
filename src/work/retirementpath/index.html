retirement investment advisors website

Retirement Investment Advisors is a fiduciary firm based in Oklahoma City. Their existing site wasn't keeping up with how they actually served clients. They needed a redesign that could organize a growing library of resources, financial tools, and market commentary, all managed by staff with no technical background.

Taxonomy system

I built a resource library using custom post types and taxonomy-driven fields through ACF. New content gets tagged and featured across the site automatically. Their financial briefs and market commentary work the same way. Staff add content and the rest is handled for them.

Reusable content

Same approach as the resource library at a page level. I created custom sections so their team can build and update pages on their own without needing a developer.

Visual refresh

Their branding elements have held steady for nearly a decade, but they were ready for a refresh in how they're applied. I kept the classic navy and gold but opened things up with warm photography, more white space, and a refreshed type system.
