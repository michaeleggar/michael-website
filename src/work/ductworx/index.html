Ductworx Website

DuctWorx is a custom ductwork fabrication company based in Oklahoma City. They needed a website that could showcase their new branding and highlight their fabrication capabilities, while also being easy for non-technical staff to update after launch.

I designed the website to align with their recently established brand identity, then built it using reusable custom sections in Elementor on WordPress so the client could manage content on their own going forward.

The layout leans into the bold colors and indsutrial photography from the rebrand, keeping the structure clean and scannable across their product categories, project gallery, and team page.
