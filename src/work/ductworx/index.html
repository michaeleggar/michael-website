Ductworx Website

DuctWorx is a ductwork fabrication company based in Oklahoma City. They needed a website that would showcase their new branding and highlight their custom fabrication capabilities. But with technical limitations on their staff, the site also needed to be easy to update and maintain.

For this project, I created a library of custom, reusable section templates, so the client can now build new pages simply by selecting sections and defining new content.

I designed the website to align with their recently established brand identity, leaning into the bold colors and their custom indsutrial photography. Although the site would not function as a shop, it was important to maintain focus on showcasing their products and team by keeping the structure clean and scannable across their product categories and work gallery.
