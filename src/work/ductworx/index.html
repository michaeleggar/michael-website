DuctWorx Website

DuctWorx, a ductwork fabrication company in Oklahoma City, needed a website that matched their new brand identity and allowed their team to handle updates without developer involvement. The challenge was building something flexible enough for non-technical editors but structured enough to stay visually consistent.

Modular ACF system

I built a library of reusable sections with ACF, each with its own content structure and display options. The client can assemble pages by choosing and populating sections without needing to touch any code.

industrial visual design

The site leans into their industrial photography and bold color palette. I kept the layout clean and scannable so the products and team stay front and center.

Self sufficient architecture

Everything from content to navigation is editable through WordPress. The goal was zero ongoing developer dependency for day-to-day updates.
